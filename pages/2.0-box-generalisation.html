Generalisation

A box with n different sides has n! specifications, because each one is a permutation of the bases, and also has n!/2 pairs of mutually reverse specifications.
  • A box with RANK different sides will have RANKS specifications, being PAIRS of mutually reverse specifications.
  • A box with RANK different sides will have RANKS specifications, being PAIRS of mutually reverse specifications.
  • A box with RANK different sides will have RANKS specifications, being PAIRS of mutually reverse specifications.
